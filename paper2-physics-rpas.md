Notes:

- Paper 2 Physics RPAs covered here are AQA GCSE Physics required practicals 6–10.
- I originally searched separate Physics first; this version also includes the relevant Trilogy/Combined Science overlaps.
- I found 6-mark practical-method questions for RPA 6, RPA 7, RPA 8, RPA 9 and RPA 10 when separate Physics and Trilogy Physics are considered together.
- RPA 7 has two useful Trilogy 6-mark method questions: a ramp-height/trolley acceleration plan from June 2020 and a varying-force trolley method from June 2023.
- RPA 8 has a Trilogy 6-mark water-wave/ripple-tank method question from June 2019, plus separate Physics shorter wave-string and ripple-tank questions.
- RPA 9 remains mainly evidenced by separate Physics practical-method questions; Trilogy has refraction concept/ray-diagram questions but I did not find a Trilogy practical-method RPA question for reflection/refraction in the data searched.
- RPA 10 has a separate Physics 6-mark emission/cooling method and useful Trilogy 4-mark infrared/Leslie-cube method/improvement questions.
