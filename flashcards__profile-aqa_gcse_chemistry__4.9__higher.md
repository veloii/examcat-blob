## How do greenhouse gases cause warming?

1. short wavelength radiation enters the atmosphere (e.g. This is mainly radiation from the Sun.)
2. radiation is absorbed by the Earth or surface
3. longer wavelength radiation is emitted (e.g. This is infrared radiation.)
4. longer wavelength radiation is absorbed by greenhouse gases and stopped from escaping (e.g. This traps heat in the atmosphere and warms the planet.)
