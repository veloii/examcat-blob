## How do you compare red-shift between galaxies?

1. greater red-shift means travelling away faster (e.g. A bigger increase in wavelength means a higher recessional speed.)
2. the galaxy travelling away faster is further away (e.g. This matches the trend that more distant galaxies recede faster.)
